The Farmer’s Patience

A farmer planted seeds in his small field at the beginning of the planting season. Every morning, he walked across the soil, checking the ground carefully. He watered the seeds faithfully, even though nothing seemed to grow at first.

Days passed, then weeks. The sun was sometimes too hot, and the rain did not always come on time. Some neighbors laughed and said he was wasting his effort because they could not see any progress. But the farmer understood something important — growth takes time beneath the surface before it becomes visible.

Even when he felt tired and doubtful, he continued caring for his field. He removed weeds, protected the soil, and trusted the process. He believed that patience and consistent effort would eventually bring results.

Then one quiet morning, he noticed tiny green shoots breaking through the soil. His heart filled with joy. The seeds had been growing all along, hidden from sight.

As the weeks went by, the small shoots turned into strong, healthy plants. The farmer smiled, knowing that good things truly take time, care, and patience. He realized that success is not instant — it is the result of steady work and unwavering faith.
